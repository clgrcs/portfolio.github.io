About Us

Hi this is Chriho a web developer student from the Cebu Eastern College based on Cebu City, Welcome to my D platform, a platform dedicated to supporting the leadership and policies of former President Rodrigo Duterte. We stand by his strong stance on law and order, crime reduction, and national progress.

Our Mission

We aim to provide fact-based insights on Duterte’s governance, particularly his fight against crime, corruption, and illegal drugs. His leadership brought:

    ✅ Lower Crime Rates – A significant decline in drug-related crimes

    ✅ Stronger Economy – Increased investments and job opportunities.

    ✅ Better Infrastructure – The “Build, Build, Build” program transformed the nation.

    ✅ Independent Foreign Policy – Prioritizing national interests on the global stage.
